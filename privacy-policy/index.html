Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

Introduction

RSS Wrangler is a browser extension designed to fetch, manage, and read RSS feeds. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect or store any personal information.
  • We do not track your browsing history or web activity.
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers.
  • All of your feed URLs, cached articles and settings are stored locally in your browser and are never sent to us.

Permissions

RSS Wrangler requires certain browser permissions (such as access to fetch data from URLs you provide) solely to perform its feed-reading functionality. These permissions are used exclusively for the extension's operation and never for data collection or tracking.

Third-Party Services

PureWord does not use third-party analytics, advertising or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
